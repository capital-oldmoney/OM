Our Philosophy

Old Money Finance was built on a single conviction: that business owners in the hardest-working sectors of the Australian economy deserve finance advice that is honest, expert, and unhurried. We are not a volume shop. We are experienced advisers who take the time to understand a business before recommending a structure.

i.
Transparency, always
Every fee, every commission, every lender option — disclosed before you commit. No surprises at settlement.
ii.
Structure over speed
The right deal structure saves more than a fast approval. We take the time to get it right.
iii.
Sector depth, not breadth
We chose three industries and learned them thoroughly. That knowledge is your competitive advantage.
iv.
Your outcome first
Decisions that cost us commission but protect you are the ones we make. That is how referrals are built.
How We Work

A measured process.
An unhurried outcome.

i

Discovery

A 30-minute conversation to understand your business, your asset needs, and your financial position — without forms or pressure.

ii

Assessment

We assess your deal honestly — including telling you when timing could be improved. Then we shortlist the right lenders from our panel.

iii

Submission

One well-prepared submission to the right lender — not a scatter-gun approach that leaves footprints on your credit file.

iv

Settlement

We manage every condition, every document, and every lender conversation until funds are disbursed and your asset is working.
